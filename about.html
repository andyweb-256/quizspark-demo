About QuizSpark
Built to make you
think harder.

QuizSpark is a lean, fast trivia game covering eight knowledge niches with four difficulty tiers and three timer modes. No accounts, no ads, no fluff — just questions.

How it works
🎯
Pick your niche

Choose from eight categories — Science & Technology, History, Geography, Pop Culture, Sports, Arts & Literature, Food & Nature, and Language & Words. Drill down into sub-topics for a more focused session.

⚖️
Set your difficulty

Four tiers: Cadet, Veteran, Elite, and Mastermind. Or choose Adaptive — the difficulty adjusts automatically based on your streak of right and wrong answers.

⏱️
Choose your pressure

Chill — no timer, think as long as you need.
Blitz — 15 seconds per question, the ring counts down.
Sudden Death — one wrong answer ends your session immediately.

📅
Daily Quiz

Every day brings a fresh set of 10 questions — the same 10 for every player worldwide, selected by a date-seeded random process. One attempt per day. Your streak grows when you play on consecutive days. Submit your score to the daily leaderboard and see where you rank.

Under the hood
Frontend Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step. Deployed to static hosting (GitHub Pages / InfinityFree).
Themes Per-niche immersive themes with layered SVG backgrounds, CSS variable overrides, parallax mouse movement, and a canvas particle engine — all custom built.
Questions Lazy-loaded per niche — only the questions you need are fetched. 107 topic leaf nodes across 8 niches. Each question has a fun fact revealed after answering.
Backend A single Cloudflare Worker proxies leaderboard reads and writes — the API key never touches the client. Streaks, preferences, and personal stats are stored locally via localStorage.
Daily The daily question set is selected client-side using a date-seeded pseudorandom number generator — no server needed to make everyone see the same questions each day.
